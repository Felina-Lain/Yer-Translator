Some grammar rules:

Words are formed by combining roots with prefixes and/or suffixes attached to the subject, the verb or the object.

There are no conjugations or gendered words. Default gender is none, and default time is present. There are no articles.

Word order is generally subject-object-verb. (Exceptions are only made for music and other forms of speech art) Adjectives follow nouns.
