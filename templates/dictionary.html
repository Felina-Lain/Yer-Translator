Some grammar rules:

Complexe words are formed by combining core and concept words together. Verbs are concept/core words with volitional prefixes.

There are no conjugations or gendered words. Default gender is none, and default time is present. There are no articles.

Word order is generally subject-object-verb. (Exceptions are only made for music and other forms of speech art) Adjectives follow nouns.

Emotions, intensity, variations and tones are indicated with pheromones. (for convenience, replaced with smileys and or signs for us)
